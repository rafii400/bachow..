What We’re About

Bachao™ is a platform of technology and people to save the helpless against rape. We developed this because we cannot take the news of another brutal rape to a helpless woman. We analysed and researched data in the past five years and the trend is upward. There is a 14% increase in rape cases every year. 76% rapes happen in the victim’s house or in its neighbourhood. We can prevent these rapes by making local people vigilant and by alerting them before such crimes occur.





We formed Bachao.Life Foundation and developed the app Bachao™. It is a non-profit initiative. It is registered, trademarked with the Government of Bangladesh. We leverage technology to engage each other to help others in emergency. We have partnered with the Government, Police, NGOs, corporates, and the communities to lead this initiative. There are many miles to go but rest assured, we will go there.
